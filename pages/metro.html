Gangchon Station

Opened in 1939, the Gyeongchun Line train connecting Seoul to Chuncheon is a train of joy for college students to MT along the Bukhan River. The train on the single track also stayed for a while to cross the oncoming train.

Piano Waterfall

The piano waterfall at the Hwado Sewage Treatment Plant in Namyangju-si, Gyeonggi-do, was made in a way that the effluent from the sewage treatment plant was pumped up and flowed down with a vertical height of 61 m and a slope length of 91.7 m.

The Garden of Morning Calm

Morning Calm Arboretum is a horticultural arboretum located at the foot of the pine forest in the dense pine forest. The arboretum is full of characteristic gardens such as Mugunghwadong Mountain, Alpine Rock Garden, Eden Garden, and trails with poetry.

Petite France

Petite France is a small town made of France, and its colorful houses and murals are attractive. This place hosts French-themed exhibitions and experience programs. In particular, the 'Little Prince Starlight Festival' is held every December.

Jara Island

Jara Island floating on one side of the Bukhan River resembles Jara. This is a natural recreation area consisting of many flowers and trees, such as a natural arboretum, sunflower square, and wild flower square. Every year in September, the Jarasum International Jazz Festival

Nami Island

Nami Island, a small island floating in the shape of a half moon on the Bukhan River. Here, there are various things to enjoy such as Metasekai Road, museum, exhibition hall, cafe, and restaurant. Nami Island is a place where you can enjoy books, culture and art together

Jade Garden

Jade Garden, an arboretum, has a wide variety of plants, including maple trees, drum flowers, and panacea. Here, there are 24 themed gardens and walking trails, so you can enjoy walking slowly. This place is famous for filming the drama [The Winter Wind Blows]

Gugok Waterfall

Gugok Falls, a 50-meter-high waterfall in Bonghwasan, is named after a waterfall that falls around nine curves. The trail from Bonghwasan Mountain to the waterfall is famous for its beautiful scenery. In addition, rock formations such as the sky wall rock are attractive around the waterfall.

Gangchon Old station

Gangchon Closed Station is a train station that was often stopped by MT or friends from the old days to come and visit Gangchon. Currently, the signboard of Gangchon Station has disappeared, and it is used as a train station for people who ride the rail bikes. Here, you can meet graffiti walls and signboards
