Opened in 1939, the Gyeongchun Line train connecting Seoul to Chuncheon is a train of joy for college students to MT along the Bukhan River. The train on the single track also stayed for a while to cross the oncoming train.

Piano Waterfall

The Gyeongchun Line, which was redoubled in 2010, has trains and limited express trains called “ITX-Youth” instead of trains. The romance of the train has been a little less, but the many famous mountains facing the North Korean River are getting closer.
